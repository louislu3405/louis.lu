Hi! I'm Louis!

Welcome to my website! I hope you enjoy everything here!

I am a passionate GIS technologist! I graduated from the University of Maryland at 2020, and now I am working as a research assistant at the Geographical Sciences Department at UMD. My interests are geosciences, remote sensing, web developement, and data sciences. When I am not working, I am either bouldering in rock climbing gyms or playing volleyballs with my friends!

Born in Taiwan, I lived and grew up on this lovely island. During my undergraduate study, I joined the mountain climbing club and hiked numerous trails, embrace the fabulous mountains in Taiwan.

This is a special year in my life. In 2017, I spent the whole summer working at Glacier National Park, Montana. I lived in the national park for 3 months, made a lot of new friends, practiced English hearing and speaking by working, and of course, enjoy my life with glaciers and mountains!

I moved from Taiwan to the States in 2019 to pursue my M.S. degree in GIS. Now, I live and work in Maryland.
